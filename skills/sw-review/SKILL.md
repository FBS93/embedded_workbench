## When to Use

- Before closing any software process activity.
- When a full review of software process work products is required.

## Critical Patterns

- Shall follow the provided resources.
- Shall ensure all software process work products are complete and consistent.
- Shall identify missing traceability, inconsistencies, or deviations from the defined software process.
- Shall verify that documentation is DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself) by adding references instead of duplicating content.
- Shall clearly state whether the software process work products are approved and the process can be closed, or if further work is needed.
